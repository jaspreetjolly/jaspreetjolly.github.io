# Which Microsoft Copilot Tool Should You Use?


## **Agents** (Automating Workflows)
- Automate repetitive tasks  
  - Approvals  
  - Notifications  
  - Data updates  
- Intelligent task assignment  
- Integrate with enterprise systems  
- Reduce errors and free time for strategic work  
- Boost productivity and operational resilience  

## **Researcher** (Deep Insights & Analysis)
- Comprehensive topic research  
- Curated, credible insights  
- Streamlined data synthesis  
- Integrates with Word & PowerPoint  
- Actionable insights for decision-making  

## **Analyst** (Data-Driven Insights)
- Visualize data: charts, graphs  
- Regression & predictive analytics  
- Integrates with Excel & Power BI  
- Converts raw data into actionable insights  
- Improves accuracy & speeds up reporting  

## **Notebook** (Structured Reasoning)
- Persistent multi-step workspace  
- Combine research, analysis & drafting  
- Break down complex tasks logically  
- Collaboration-friendly environment  
- Enhances planning & decision quality  

## **Chat** (Quick Answers & Brainstorming)
- Instant answers & clarifications  
- Supports brainstorming & idea refinement  
- Integrated into Teams & Outlook  
- Fast, flexible, productivity-focused  

## **Search** (Finding Enterprise & Web Content)
- Unified access across SharePoint, OneDrive, Outlook  
- Extends to web content  
- Advanced filtering: date, author, file type  
- Accelerates decision-making through fast information retrieval  

## **Create** (Generating Original Content)
- Create documents, presentations, visuals  
- Templates & customization  
- Compliance & standards alignment  
- Faster content creation with high impact
